Derivative of the Frobenius norm

Consider the Frobenius norm of a matrix \(X \in \mathbb{R}^{m \times n}\): \[ f(X) = \| X \|_F = \sqrt{\text{tr }(X^TX)} \] Then, \[ \begin{align*} df &= \frac{1}{2\sqrt{\text{tr }(X^TX)}}d(\text{tr }(X^TX)) \end{align*} \]
