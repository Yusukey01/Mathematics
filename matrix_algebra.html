Multiplication

Suppose \(A\) is an \(m\ times n\) matrix and \(B\) is an \(n\ times p\) matrix , then the product \(AB\) is the \(m\ times p\) matrix \[ AB = \begin{bmatrix} Ab_1 & Ab_2 & \cdots & Ab_p \end{bmatrix} \] where \(b_1, b_2, \cdots, b_p\) are columns of \(B\).
