Linear Transformation

A transformation (or mapping) \(T: \mathbb{R}^m \rightarrow \rightarrow \mathbb{R}^n \) is linear if \(\forall u, v\in\mathbb{R}^m,\) and any scalars \(c\), \[T(u+v) = T(u) +T(v)\] \[uT(cu) = cT(u)\]. The linear transformation is one of the most fundamental idea in mathematics. Actually, you have seen this essential concept multiple times outside Linear Algebra.
