Linear Algebra

  Linear Algebra is one of the most fundamental areas of modern mathematics. Scientists and engineers widely appreciate its powerful applications. In linear algebra, understanding the concepts is often more important than performing computations by hand as large-scale calculations are typically handled by computers. Also, linear algebra is a part of the modern algebra also known as abstract algebra, which provides a "common language" across branches of mathematics. For those who love pure pure mathematics, learning linear algebra can be a great introduction to the wworld of modern mathematics.
